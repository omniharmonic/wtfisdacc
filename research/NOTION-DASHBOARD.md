## The Core Thesis

Technologies that shift the offense/defense balance toward defense—without centralizing power—represent both:

1. **The highest-alignment opportunities for human flourishing**
2. **Undervalued market positions** due to systematic under-indexing by traditional capital allocators

The market over-indexes on: L1 blockchains, enterprise solutions, centralized stablecoins, consumer NFTs

The market under-indexes on: Privacy-preserving computation, identity infrastructure, compliance-preserving privacy, democratic coordination mechanisms

# The Four D's Framework

Every investment candidate must pass this filter:

| | Criterion | Core Question | Red Flag |
|---|-----------|---------------|----------|
| **D** | Defensive | Does this shift power toward protection rather than attack? | Creates new attack surfaces |
| **D** | Decentralized | Can this operate without a single controlling entity? | Requires trust in "good guys" staying good |
| **D** | Democratic | Does this enable broader participation in decisions? | Concentrates capability in small groups |
| **D** | Differential | Does this accelerate defense faster than offense? | Equally useful for harmful applications |

> **The Key Insight:** A technology can score well on three criteria and still fail. All four must hold—and the defense mechanism itself cannot become a new vector for centralization.

### What Disqualifies a Technology

- Requiring trust that centralized actors will remain benevolent
- Creating new attack surfaces that didn't exist before
- Enabling cheaper harm than protection
- Facilitating surveillance or control infrastructure
- Concentrating capability in ways that could be weaponized

# Vitalik's Defense Categories

The d/acc framework identifies four domains where defensive technology matters:

## Cyber Defense ⟠
*Where most on-chain primitives live*

Blockchains, zero-knowledge proofs, cryptocurrencies, social recovery wallets, trusted hardware, hardened operating systems.

> *"Blockchains let us create economic and social structures with a 'shared hard drive' without having to depend on centralized actors."*

**Key Primitives:** ZK Proofs, DID, Rollups, Key Management, Privacy Computation

---

## Info Defense ◈
*Coordination without pre-assumed authorities*

Community Notes-style fact-checking, prediction markets, consensus-finding algorithms, wallet-level scam detection, ZK-based identity.

> *"The key criterion: avoiding pre-assumed authorities that 'know what is true and false' and enforce perspectives on everyone."*

**Key Primitives:** Sybil Resistance, Voting Mechanisms, Quadratic Funding, Impact Certificates

---

## Micro Physical Defense (Bio) ✧
*Public health woven into civilization's fabric*

Far-UVC irradiation, air filtering, rapid vaccine platforms, wastewater surveillance, decentralized health infrastructure.

> *"A future that doesn't have to resort to the sledgehammer of social compulsion because the infrastructure of public health is woven into the fabric of civilization."*

**Investment Note:** This domain is largely outside crypto/blockchain. Worth discussing: should d/acc capital allocation extend beyond on-chain primitives?

---

## Macro Physical Defense ◇
*Resilient infrastructure without central dependency*

Decentralized connectivity (Starlink), independent energy (home solar), resilient supply chains, multi-planetary civilization.

> *"Building tools to help people survive and live comfortable lives independently or semi-independently of long international supply chains."*

**Investment Note:** Also largely outside crypto scope. Potential intersection: decentralized energy markets, supply chain verification.
