# Insurance

**D/ACC Alignment**: 4 | **Transformation Timeline**: 3-5 Years
**Current Market**: Emerging
**2030+ Projection**: $40.6B by 2033

## Overview

Parametric insurance, claims automation, and reinsurance. Arbol provides AI-powered weather insurance with Chainlink oracles. dRe Platform launched first on-chain parametric reinsurance. Etherisc handles flight delays and crop insurance.

- Parametric insurance is purely defensive

- Oracles enable trustless verification
- Aligns with [[Micro Physical Defense]] (disaster recovery)

## How Parametric Insurance Works

1. Policy defines trigger conditions (e.g., rainfall < X inches)
2. [[Oracle Infrastructure]] monitors conditions
3. Smart contract automatically pays out if triggered
4. No claims process, no human judgment
