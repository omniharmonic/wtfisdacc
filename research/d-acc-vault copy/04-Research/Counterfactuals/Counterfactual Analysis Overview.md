# Counterfactual Analysis Overview

Vacuum analysis: What happens if D/ACC-aligned investment doesn't flow to a primitive? What fills the vacuum, and how does that affect the offense/defense balance?

This analysis helps prioritize investment by understanding the **strategic implications of inaction**.

## Framework

For each primitive, we ask:
1. **If we don't invest, what happens?**
2. **What fills the vacuum?**
3. **What's the D/ACC alignment of the vacuum filler?**
4. **Net impact of inaction?**
