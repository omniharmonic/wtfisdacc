## Evidence Evaluation Criteria

For each claim:
1. **Source** - Who made the claim?
2. **Verifiability** - Can it be independently checked?
3. **Incentive alignment** - Does the source benefit from overstating?
4. **Historical accuracy** - Has this source been reliable?
5. **What would disprove?** - What evidence would invalidate?

## Investment Implications

**Validated claims** → Weight heavily in analysis
**Partially validated** → Monitor for confirmation
**Weakly validated** → Discount appropriately
**Projections** → Use as scenarios, not forecasts
