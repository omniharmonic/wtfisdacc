## Overview

Decentralized storage for rollup data, enabling light node verification without downloading full blocks. DA costs represent 90%+ of L2 operating expenses, making this a critical infrastructure bottleneck.

## Why D/ACC Aligned

Decentralizes the critical DA bottleneck for rollup scaling. Data Availability Sampling (DAS) enables light nodes to verify without trusting validators—preventing validator centralization through cryptographic guarantees.
