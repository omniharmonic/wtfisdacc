# Decentralized Identity (DID)

**Category**: Identity > DID
**D/ACC Score**: 5 | **Investment Tier**: [[Tier 1 - Highest Conviction]]
**Development Stage**: Early Growth | **Risk Profile**: Medium-High
**Defense Category**: [[Cyber Defense]]

## Overview

Self-sovereign identity with 200+ DID methods published. W3C standards maturing. Buenos Aires deployed ZK identity for 3.6M citizens—demonstrating government-scale feasibility.

## Why D/ACC Aligned

Creates self-sovereign alternatives to platform-controlled credentials. Individuals own and selectively disclose identity without depending on corporations or governments. This is a direct counter to AI-enabled surveillance.

**[[Decentralized Identity Thesis]]**

## Counterfactual Analysis

**What if self-sovereign identity doesn't achieve adoption?**
- Government digital ID and Big Tech providers become default
- **Vacuum filled by**: Apple/Google passkeys, EU digital identity wallet, China-style social credit
- **Impact**: Strongly Negative - identity becomes control infrastructure
