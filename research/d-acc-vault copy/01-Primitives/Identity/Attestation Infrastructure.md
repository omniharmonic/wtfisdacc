## Overview

On-chain credential issuance and verification infrastructure. [[EAS]] (Ethereum Attestation Service) enables 80% compliance cost reduction and 90% faster verification compared to paper-based systems.

## Why D/ACC Aligned

Enables portable, verifiable credentials without reliance on centralized issuers. Attestations can come from multiple sources, creating redundancy and reducing single points of failure.
