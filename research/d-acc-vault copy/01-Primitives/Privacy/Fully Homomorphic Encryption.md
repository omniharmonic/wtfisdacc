# Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE)

**Category**: Privacy > FHE
**D/ACC Score**: 5 | **Investment Tier**: [[Tier 1 - Highest Conviction]]
**Development Stage**: Very Early | **Risk Profile**: Very High
**Defense Category**: [[Cyber Defense]]

## Overview

Computation on encrypted data. [[Zama]] achieved unicorn status ($130M). Current performance is 20 TPS, with targets of 1,000 TPS using ASIC hardware acceleration. FHE represents "the final frontier of on-chain privacy."

## Why D/ACC Aligned

Enables encrypted data processing, preventing AI systems from accessing raw information. Unlike [[Zero-Knowledge Proofs]] (which prove statements), FHE allows actual computation on encrypted inputs—more composable for multi-party scenarios.
