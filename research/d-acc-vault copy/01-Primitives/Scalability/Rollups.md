## Overview

Optimistic rollups (83-85% share, 7-day finality) vs ZK rollups (15-17%, instant validity proofs). L2s process transactions off-chain while inheriting L1 security. ZK rollups are growing rapidly.

## Why D/ACC Aligned

Scales blockchain without sacrificing decentralization. Users maintain L1 security guarantees while getting higher throughput and lower costs. ZK rollups additionally preserve privacy.
