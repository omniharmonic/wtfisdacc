## Why D/ACC Aligned

Demonstrates that democratic coordination works. Small contributions are mathematically amplified—$1 from 100 people generates more matching than $100 from 1 person.

## Mechanism

1. Projects apply for grants round
2. Contributors donate (any amount)
3. Matching pool distributed via QF formula: (Σ√contributions)² - Σcontributions
4. More unique contributors = more matching

## Sybil Resistance

Uses [[Gitcoin Passport]] to prevent gaming:
- Multi-signal verification
- Minimum score requirement
- Forgery-cost-based weights
