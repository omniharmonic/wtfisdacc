## About This Research

This vault synthesizes research into defensive accelerationism—a framework for evaluating technology that:
- Strengthens **defense** over offense
- **Distributes** rather than concentrates power
- Enables **democratic** participation
- Grows defensive capability **faster** than offensive (differential)

The research is empirically grounded in actual d/acc community practice (Devcon Bangkok, ETH SF, SHIFT Grants, Balvi Fund) while maintaining theoretical coherence with Vitalik's original framework.
