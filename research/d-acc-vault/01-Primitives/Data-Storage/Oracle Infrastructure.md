## Overview

Off-chain data feeds bringing real-world information on-chain. [[Chainlink]] maintains 46-58% market share. [[Pyth]] grew 46x in 2024. Chronicle offers 61% cheaper operation.

## Why D/ACC Concern

Critical middleware but market concentration creates D/ACC tension. Chainlink's dominance means a single point of failure for much of DeFi. Emerging networks with decentralized architecture are preferred.
