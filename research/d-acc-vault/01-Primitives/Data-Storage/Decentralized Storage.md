# Decentralized Storage

**Category**: Data/Storage > Storage
**D/ACC Score**: 4 | **Investment Tier**: [[Tier 2 - Strong Conviction]]
**Development Stage**: Growth | **Risk Profile**: Medium
**Defense Category**: [[Cyber Defense]]

## Overview

[[Filecoin]] offers 22 exabytes capacity with incentivized storage. [[Arweave]] provides permanent storage at $9-12/GB for ~200-year storage. Combined, they show 414% storage growth.

## Why D/ACC Aligned

Ensures data availability without platform dependency. Prevents AI/corporations from controlling information access. Data persists even if original publisher disappears.

## Key Implementations

| Project | Model | Capacity |
