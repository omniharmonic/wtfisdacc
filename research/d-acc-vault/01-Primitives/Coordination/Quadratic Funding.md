## Overview

Mathematically amplifies small contributions. $60M+ distributed via [[Gitcoin]], with 400x matching multipliers demonstrated for small donations. Enables collective prioritization of public goods without centralized allocation.

## Why D/ACC Aligned

Democratic coordination primitive that counters plutocracy. The math: matching = (Σ√contributions)² - Σcontributions. This means $1 from 100 people generates more matching than $100 from 1 person.
