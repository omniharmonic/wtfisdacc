# Impact Certificates (Hypercerts)

**Category**: Coordination > Impact Verification
**D/ACC Score**: 4 | **Investment Tier**: [[Tier 2 - Strong Conviction]]
**Development Stage**: Early | **Risk Profile**: High
**Defense Category**: [[Info Defense]]

## Overview

Tokenized impact claims (extended ERC-1155) encoding scope and contributors. 10,000+ [[Hypercerts]] in circulation with 20,000+ unique addresses participating.

## Why D/ACC Aligned

Enables retroactive funding of public goods with verifiable impact. Creates market for impact that doesn't require upfront prediction of what will be valuable.

## Key Implementations

| Project | Status | Integration |
|---------|--------|-------------|
| [[Hypercerts]] | 10K+ issued | EasyRetroPGF integration |
| EasyRetroPGF | Active | Streamlined retroactive funding |

## Investment Thesis

Early-stage primitive enabling new coordination models. Part of broader retroactive public goods funding ecosystem pioneered by [[Optimism]].

## Mechanism

1. Project creates hypercert claiming impact
2. Hypercert specifies: work scope, timeframe, contributors
3. Retrospective funders purchase fractions
4. Creates market price for demonstrated impact

## D/ACC Properties

- **Democratic**: Anyone can claim and verify impact
- **Decentralized**: No central committee decides value
- **Defensive**: Incentivizes public goods creation
