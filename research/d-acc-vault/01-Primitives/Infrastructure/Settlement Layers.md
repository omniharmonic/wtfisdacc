## Overview

Base layer blockchains providing final transaction validity and security. These are the foundational layers upon which all other crypto infrastructure is built.

## Why D/ACC Aligned

Provides permissionless, censorship-resistant settlement without trusted parties. No single entity can prevent or reverse transactions once finalized.
