# Voting Mechanisms

**Category**: Governance > Voting
**D/ACC Score**: 4 | **Investment Tier**: [[Tier 2 - Strong Conviction]]
**Development Stage**: Growth | **Risk Profile**: Medium
**Defense Category**: [[Info Defense]]

## Overview

On-chain governance with multiple mechanism variants: token-based, quadratic, conviction, and holographic consensus. Average participation remains only 17-28%, indicating significant room for improvement.

## Why D/ACC Aligned

Enables broader participation in decisions. Quadratic voting reduces plutocratic dominance through square-root weighting—$1 from 100 people outweighs $100 from 1 person.

## Voting Mechanism Types

| Type | Mechanism | D/ACC Alignment |
|------|-----------|-----------------|
| Token-weighted | 1 token = 1 vote | Low (plutocratic) |
| Quadratic | Vote power = √tokens | High (democratic) |
| Conviction | Time-weighted voting | Medium |
| Holographic | Prediction markets + voting | High |
