## Overview

Distributed key management and computation across parties without revealing inputs. Multiple parties can jointly compute a function while keeping their individual inputs private.

## Why D/ACC Aligned

Eliminates single points of failure in key custody. Enables cooperation without revealing sensitive data—no single party has complete information or control.
