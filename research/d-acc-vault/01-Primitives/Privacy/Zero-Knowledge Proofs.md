## Overview

Verification without trust. ZK proofs allow one party to prove a statement is true without revealing any information beyond the validity of the statement itself. The market is $1.28B (2024) projected to $7.59B by 2033 (22.1% CAGR).

## Why D/ACC Aligned

> "The only known technology allowing verification without trust, privacy without central authority, compliance without surveillance."

ZK proofs are uniquely positioned to address the core D/ACC concern: how do we maintain privacy in a world of increasing AI surveillance capability?
