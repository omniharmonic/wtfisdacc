## Overview

Privacy-preserving credential verification—prove attributes without revealing information. [[Semaphore]] powers [[World ID]]. This primitive enables the seemingly contradictory goals of verification AND privacy.

## Why D/ACC Aligned

Directly counters Vitalik's concern: "with modern AI to interpret it, there may be no place to hide." ZK identity allows proving claims (age, citizenship, credentials) without revealing the underlying data.

## Investment Thesis

**[[Decentralized Identity Thesis]]**

Unique capability—privacy and compliance become compatible rather than mutually exclusive. Enables:
- KYC without data exposure
- Age verification without ID scanning
- Credit checks without full history

## Counterfactual Analysis

**What if ZK identity doesn't scale?**
- Identity verification requires full disclosure
- Privacy/compliance tradeoff unresolved
- **Vacuum filled by**: KYC everywhere, identity-linked transactions, financial surveillance
- **Impact**: Strongly Negative - privacy and compliance become mutually exclusive

## Temporal Dynamics

**[[Privacy Regulatory Pressure]]**
- Increasing regulatory hostility to privacy technologies
- Tornado Cash precedent creates risk
- ZK projects adding compliance features defensively
