# Intent-Based Systems

## Overview

Solver competition for optimal execution. [[CoW Protocol]] commands 63% of intent trading with $2.4B monthly volume. Users express *what* they want (intent), solvers compete on *how* to execute.

## Why D/ACC Aligned

Redirects MEV (Miner Extractable Value) back to users as price improvement. Instead of bots extracting value from users, competitive solvers pass savings to users.
