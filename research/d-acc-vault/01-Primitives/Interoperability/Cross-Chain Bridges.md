## Overview

~$1.4B TVL despite $2.8B+ historical exploit losses (40% of Web3 hacks). ZK-based bridges are emerging as safer alternatives to trusted validator sets.

## Why D/ACC Concern

Necessary for ecosystem connectivity but centralization tendencies create caution. Most bridges rely on small multisigs or validator sets—identifiable chokepoints for regulatory capture or attack.

## Why ZK Bridges Are D/ACC Aligned

ZK-based variants use validity proofs instead of trust assumptions—verification without trusting the bridge operators.
