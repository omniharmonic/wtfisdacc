# Defense Categories

D/ACC organizes the defensive technology landscape into four interconnected domains, spanning both physical and digital realms.

## Macro Physical Defense (Atoms-Macro)

**Domain**: Resilient infrastructure, decentralized connectivity, independent energy, multi-planetary civilization.

Technologies that help people survive and live comfortable lives independently or semi-independently of long international supply chains.

**Key Technologies:**
- Starlink
- Home Solar
- Resilient Infrastructure
- Space Technology

**D/ACC Alignment**: 4

> "Building tools to help people survive and even live comfortable lives independently or semi-independently of long international supply chains seems like a valuable defensive technology." — Vitalik Buterin

---

## Micro Physical Defense (Atoms-Micro)

**Domain**: Far-UVC irradiation, air filtering, rapid vaccine platforms, wastewater surveillance, decentralized health infrastructure.

Creating public health infrastructure woven into the fabric of civilization without requiring social compulsion.

**Key Technologies:**
- Far-UVC
- Air Filtering
- Open Source Vaccines
- Epidemic Detection
- Wastewater Surveillance

**D/ACC Alignment**: 5

> "A future that doesn't have to resort to the sledgehammer of social compulsion because the infrastructure of public health is woven into the fabric of civilization." — Vitalik Buterin

---

## Cyber Defense (Bits-Cyber)

**Domain**: Blockchains, [[Zero-Knowledge Proofs]], cryptocurrencies, [[Social Recovery Wallets]], trusted hardware chips, hardened operating systems.

Economic and social structures with a shared hard drive without centralized dependency.

**Key Technologies:**
- [[Zero-Knowledge Proofs]]
- Blockchains
- Cryptography
- Account Abstraction
- Social Recovery
- Trusted Hardware

**D/ACC Alignment**: 5

> "Blockchains let us create economic and social structures with a 'shared hard drive' without having to depend on centralized actors." — Vitalik Buterin

**Related Primitives:**
- [[Consensus Mechanisms]]
- [[Settlement Layers]]
- [[Rollups]]
- [[Fully Homomorphic Encryption]]
- [[MPC]]
- [[Key Management]]

---

## Info Defense (Bits-Info)

**Domain**: Community Notes-style crowdsourced fact-checking, [[Prediction Markets]], consensus-finding algorithms (Pol.is), wallet-level scam detection, ZK-based identity systems.

Avoiding pre-assumed authorities that "know what is true and false" and enforce perspectives on everyone.

**Key Technologies:**
- Community Notes
- [[Prediction Markets]]
- Pol.is
- [[Sybil Resistance]]
- Scam Detection

**D/ACC Alignment**: 4

> "The key criterion: avoiding pre-assumed authorities that 'know what is true and false' and enforce perspectives on everyone." — Vitalik Buterin

**Related Primitives:**
- [[Reputation and Sybil Resistance]]
- [[Voting Mechanisms]]
- [[Quadratic Funding]]
