# Information Resilience & Epistemics MOC

*"The key criterion: avoiding pre-assumed authorities that 'know what is true and false' and enforce perspectives on everyone."* — Vitalik

**Quadrant**: Bits-Survive / Bits-Thrive
**Primary Primitives**: [[VERIFY]], [[COORDINATE]], [[STORE]]

---

## The D/ACC Info Defense Vision

Traditional information systems:
- Centralized fact-checkers (who watches the watchmen?)
- Platform algorithms optimizing for engagement (→ polarization)
- Expert authorities (subject to capture)

D/ACC information resilience:
- Crowdsourced verification (Community Notes)
- Skin-in-the-game truth-finding (Prediction Markets)
- Consensus-surfacing algorithms (Pol.is)
- Cryptographic provenance (zkEmail, Proteus)

## Why Info Defense Is D/ACC Core

1. **Defensive**: Protects against misinformation without censorship
2. **Decentralized**: No central authority on truth
3. **Democratic**: Anyone can participate in verification
4. **Differential**: Truth-finding scales; manipulation has limits
