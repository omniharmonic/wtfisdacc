## The D/ACC Biodefense Vision

Traditional pandemic response relies on:
- Social compulsion (lockdowns, mandates)
- Centralized institutions (CDC, WHO)
- Slow vaccine development (10-15 years traditional)
- Reactive measures

D/ACC biodefense weaves public health into civilization's fabric:
- Environmental pathogen elimination (Far-UVC)
- Open-source rapid response (RaDVaC)
- Decentralized detection (wastewater, air sensors)
- Personal-scale medical devices (OpenWater)

## Why Biosecurity Is D/ACC Core

1. **Defensive by nature**: Protecting health is pure defense
2. **Decentralizable**: Open-source vaccines, personal devices
3. **Democratic**: Everyone benefits from pathogen elimination
4. **Differential**: Defense (immunity) scales; offense (bioweapons) is harder
