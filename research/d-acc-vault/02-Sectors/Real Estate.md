# Real Estate

**D/ACC Alignment**: 4 | **Transformation Timeline**: 3-5 Years
**Current Market**: $3.5B fractional ownership (2024)
**2030+ Projection**: $19.4B by 2033 (fractional) | **CAGR**: 21%

## Overview

Land registries, fractional ownership, and property tokenization. Georgia has 100,000+ titles on Bitcoin blockchain. Sweden has 1M+ properties on ChromaWay. Dubai uses XRP Ledger for land registry.

## Key Implementations

| Implementation | Scale | Notes |
|----------------|-------|-------|
| Georgia | 100,000+ titles on Bitcoin, 2M+ records | Government deployment |
| Sweden | 1M+ properties (ChromaWay) | Lantmäteriet partnership |
| Dubai | XRP Ledger | Land Department |
| RealT | 2,500+ properties, 40,000+ investors | Fractional platform |
