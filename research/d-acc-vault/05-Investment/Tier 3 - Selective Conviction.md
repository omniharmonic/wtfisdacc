# Tier 3 - Selective Conviction

**Position Sizing**: Selective/Speculative

Tier 3 represents context-dependent [[Four Ds Framework|D/ACC alignment]] (★★★☆☆ or lower) requiring careful evaluation of specific implementations. May be critical infrastructure with concentration concerns or emerging categories with unclear trajectories.

## Criteria

- **D/ACC Alignment Threshold**: 3 or below
- **Market Opportunity**: Variable - may have large markets but d/acc alignment is implementation-specific
- Passes some [[Four Ds Framework|Four D's]] criteria
- Important infrastructure but centralization tendencies
- May require monitoring for trajectory changes
- Vacuum analysis may justify despite lower alignment

## Risk Tolerance

High - Accept concentration risk or unclear D/ACC trajectory for strategic infrastructure positions.

## Portfolio Allocation

Smaller allocation; highly selective; position sizing based on specific implementation quality; may be traded rather than held.
