# Investment Thesis MOC (Map of Content)

Strategic investment frameworks for D/ACC-aligned capital allocation.

## Tier 1 Thesis Areas

### [[Zero-Knowledge Infrastructure Thesis]]
**Summary**: Universal privacy + compliance compatibility creates massive market opportunity

- Market: $90B ZK L2 by 2031 (60.7% CAGR)
- Key vectors: zkEVM Rollups, ZK Credentials, ZK Proving, Developer Tools
- Projects: [[zkSync]], [[StarkNet]], [[Scroll]], [[Aztec Network]]
- Validation: Enterprise pilots (Deutsche Bank, HSBC) converting to production

### [[Decentralized Identity Thesis]]
**Summary**: Foundation for institutional adoption while preserving user sovereignty

- Market: Multi-trillion across finance, healthcare, governance
- Key vectors: Attestation (EAS), ZK Credentials, Reputation, Social Recovery
- Projects: [[Polygon ID]], [[Gitcoin Passport]], [[EAS]], [[ENS]]
- Validation: Buenos Aires 3.6M citizens, 80% compliance cost reduction

### [[Privacy-Preserving Computation Thesis]]
**Summary**: Institutional privacy gap creates demand-supply mismatch

- Market: ~$250M funded vs multi-trillion opportunity
- Key vectors: FHE Platforms, MPC Wallets, FHE Hardware
- Projects: [[Zama]], [[Aztec Network]], [[Lit Protocol]]
- Validation: Zama unicorn status, FHE performance trajectory

## Tier 2 Thesis Areas

### [[Data Availability Thesis]]
**Summary**: Infrastructure bottleneck removal for rollup scaling

- Market: Scales with rollup adoption
- Key vectors: DA Layer Tokens, Rollup Infrastructure
- Projects: [[Celestia]], [[EigenDA]], [[Avail]]
- Validation: Celestia cost advantage vs EigenDA security

### [[Quadratic Funding Thesis]]
**Summary**: Democratic coordination primitive demonstrating sustainable public goods financing

- Market: $60M+ distributed, $100M+ committed
- Key vectors: Funding Protocol Infrastructure, Sybil Resistance, Impact Verification
- Projects: [[Gitcoin]], [[Allo Protocol]], [[Hypercerts]]
- Validation: QV adoption correlation with participation

### [[Decentralized Stablecoin Thesis]]
**Summary**: Reduce concentration risk in largest crypto market

- Market: $309B, 91% centralized
- Key vectors: Decentralized Protocols, Yield Optimization, Delta-Neutral
- Projects: [[DAI]], [[Ethena]]
- Validation: Peg stability under stress

## Tier 3 Thesis Areas

### [[Oracle Infrastructure Thesis]]
**Summary**: Critical middleware with decentralization trajectory opportunity

- Market: All sectors needing off-chain data
- Key vectors: Emerging networks with decentralized architecture
- Projects: [[Pyth]], Chronicle, RedStone (vs [[Chainlink]])
- Validation: Market share dynamics

### [[ZK Bridge Thesis]]
**Summary**: Security resolution for cross-chain with reduced trust

- Market: Essential multi-chain infrastructure
- Key vectors: ZK-Based Bridges, Intent Systems
- Projects: [[LayerZero]] V2, [[CoW Protocol]]
- Validation: ZK bridge security in production
