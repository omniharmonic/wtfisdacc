# OpenWater

**Stage**: Production 2025 | **Funding**: $54M

## Overview

OpenWater is creating wearable MRI-quality medical imaging at consumer electronics prices. Founded by Mary Lou Jepsen (former Facebook/Oculus/Google/Intel executive), the technology uses infrared holography to achieve imaging that is "1000x cheaper than an MRI and a billion times higher resolution."

## Technology

- Uses liquid crystal displays (LCDs) and near infrared (NIR) light
- Scans 5-6 inches deep into the body at micron resolution (same size as a neuron)
- Can fit into a ski hat, bandage, or piece of clothing
- AGPL licensed open source platform

## Applications

- Cancer detection (spotting tumors via surrounding blood vessels)
- Clogged artery detection
- Internal bleeding monitoring
- Brain disease monitoring (mental and neurodegenerative)
- Depression treatment (stunning remission results reported)
- Glioblastoma treatment (mouse studies)
