## Overview

VitaDAO is a community-owned decentralized autonomous organization dedicated to funding early-stage longevity research. Launched July 2021, it has funded 24 projects totaling $4.2M in an area traditionally underfunded by risk-averse institutions.

## Why DeSci for Longevity?

Compared to cancer or Alzheimer's research, longevity science is severely underfunded. Traditional funding agencies are too risk-averse for transformative "moonshot" science. VitaDAO addresses this gap through:

- Community governance via VITA token
- IP-NFT tokenization (via Molecule platform)
- Direct researcher-to-community funding
