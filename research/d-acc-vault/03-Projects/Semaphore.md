## Overview

Anonymous group membership signaling—powers [[World ID]]'s proof of personhood. Ethereum Foundation PSE (Privacy & Scaling Explorations) project.

## Key Differentiators

- **Privacy-preserving group membership** - Prove you're in a group without revealing who you are
- **Powers World ID** - Core protocol for proof-of-personhood
- **Ethereum Foundation backed** - PSE team development

## How It Works

1. User joins a group (receives identity commitment)
2. User can signal anonymously as group member
3. ZK proof shows: "I am in this group" without revealing which member
4. Prevents double-signaling while preserving anonymity

## Why D/ACC Aligned

Foundational privacy primitive enabling:
- Anonymous voting
- Whistleblower protection
- Private group coordination
