## Overview

Multi-signal identity verification aggregating web2/web3 credentials. Acquired by Holonym Foundation. Protects $380M+ in funds with 2M+ users and 34M+ credentials.

## Key Differentiators

- **Stamps system** - Multiple credential sources aggregated
- **Forgery-cost-based weights** - Harder to fake = higher score
- **Minimum score threshold** - 20 points for Gitcoin participation

## How It Works

1. User collects "stamps" from various sources
2. Each stamp has weight based on forgery difficulty
3. Combined score indicates unique humanness
4. Score used for [[Quadratic Funding]] and governance
