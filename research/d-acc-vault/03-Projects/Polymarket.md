# Polymarket

**Stage**: Deployed | **Funding**: $70M+

## Overview

Polymarket is the world's largest decentralized prediction market, built on Polygon. Founded in 2020 by Shayne Coplan, it allows users to bet on real-world event outcomes. In 2024, it processed $9B in trading volume with 300,000+ users.

## 2024 Election Performance

Polymarket famously outperformed traditional polls in predicting the 2024 US presidential election:
- Gave Trump 58% odds when polls showed ~50-50
- $3.2B wagered on presidential race alone
- Closer to final Electoral College tally than mainstream forecasts
- Wall Street firms (including JP Morgan) monitored the site's odds

## Why It Works

- **Skin in the game**: Financial incentives for accuracy
- **Aggregates diverse information**: Market efficiency
- **Reduces personal bias**: "Shy voters" more honest when money involved
- **Real-time updates**: Faster than polling

## Limitations

- Not foolproof (Hillary Clinton was favored in 2016)
- Vulnerable to wash trading (estimated up to 1/3 of volume in 2024)
- Regulatory issues: $1.4M CFTC fine in 2022
