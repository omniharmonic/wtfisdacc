# World ID

**Category**: ZK Identity
**D/ACC Score**: 4 | **Investment Tier**: [[Tier 1 - Highest Conviction]]
**Stage**: Mainnet | **Token**: WLD

## Overview

Biometric proof-of-personhood with ZK privacy using [[Semaphore]] protocol. Part of Worldcoin ($240M+ raised). 9,000+ project integrations.

## Key Differentiators

- **Biometric uniqueness** - Iris scanning for unique personhood
- **ZK privacy** - Prove personhood without revealing identity
- **9,000+ integrations** - Growing ecosystem

## How It Works

1. User scans iris with Orb device
2. Biometric creates unique identifier
3. ZK proof enables verification without revealing iris data
4. User proves "I am a unique human" without revealing who

## Why D/ACC Aligned

Enables proof-of-personhood without centralized identity authority. Critical for:
- [[Quadratic Funding]] participation
- Bot-resistant voting
- Fair airdrops

## D/ACC Considerations

Score of 4 (not 5) because:
- Orb hardware creates centralization concerns
- Biometric data collection is sensitive
- Worldcoin entity has significant control
